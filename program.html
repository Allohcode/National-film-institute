Diplom and Degree Programmes

This to inform all interested film enthusiasts that our diploma and degree programmes include courses in:

  • Cinematography
  • Film Editing
  • Scriptwriting
  • Digital Still Photography
  • Acting
  • Directing
  • Animation
  • Costume, Makeup and Special Effects

The programmes are designed to give participants intensive classroom experience and hands-on training with state-of-the-art equipment.

Genaral Entry Requiments

Candidates applying for the Diploma or Bachelor of Film Arts must obtain credit in English Language, Literature in English, Physics and any other four subjects in the humanities at not more than two (2) sittings, in addition to at least a pass in Mathematics at SSCE, GCE '0' Level.

Four-Year-Degree Programme

Applicants must write the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) and obtain the approved scores in the Use of English (compulsory) and three other subjects.

Two-Years Diploma Programme

Applicants must write the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) and obtain the approved scores in the Use of English (compulsory) and three other subjects.

Certificate Course

The Certificate program is run quaterly by the Institute spanning between 6 to 12weeks. Reminder adverts are usually place online and other media for this program.

Outreach Programme

The Outreach Programme is usually organized for willing institutions and corporate organizations on demand.
